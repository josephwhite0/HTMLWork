CIT 150 INTERNET TECHNOLOGIES

The Internet Technologies class provides students with a study of traditional and emerging Internet Technologies. It covers topics including Internet fundamentals, internet applications, Internet delivery systems, and Internet client/server computing. It also provides a hands-on experience and some rudimentary programming in an Internet environment.

Here are the sub-topics covered in the course:

  1. A Brief History of the Internet
  2. Inside the Internet
  3. How people use the Internet Hyper Text Markup Language JavaScript
  4. Cascading Syle Sheets
